Shipping

We offer worldwide shipping to all our customers. Our shipping policies are designed to ensure that you receive your orders in a timely and efficient manner.

Shipping Methods

We provide various shipping options, including standard shipping and express shipping. The delivery time may vary depending on your location and the shipping method selected.

Delivery Times

  • Standard Shipping: 7-14 business days
  • Express Shipping: 3-7 business days

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on the weight of your order and the destination. You can view the shipping charges during the checkout process.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this tracking number to check the status of your delivery on our website.

Customs and Import Duties

Please note that customs and import duties may apply to international orders. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient and may vary depending on your country's regulations.
